How to Wander

Move — hold your mouse / finger to gently pull Wispy toward it. Or use arrow keys / WASD.

Dewdrops — collect every glowing droplet. Each one tints Wispy a new color and gives ✨ points.

Portal — when the portal glows a target color (from dream 8 onward), match Wispy's color before entering.

Soft clouds — they gently bounce you. They never hurt.

Grumpfs — the grumpy red puffs from dream 5 onward. Touching one costs a ♥. You have 3 hearts!

Exit anytime — tap ☰ Menu (top right) or press Esc.
